Rhubarb Pie

Description

Easy Rhubarb Pie recipe that is tangy, buttery and delicious. Great with a scoop of vanilla icecream!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of Rhubarb
  • 1 cup of Sugar
  • 1 cup of Brown Sugar
  • 1/2 cup of Butter
  • 1 Pie Crust

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (204) C
  2. Line your pie crust in your pie dish and brush with butter
  3. Combine rhubarb, sugar, brown sugar in mixing bowl
  4. Pour mixture into pie crust
  5. cover with pie crust and sprinkle brown sugar on top
  6. Bake for 20 minutes
  7. Reduce heat to 350 F (177 C) after 20 minutes and bake for additional 30 minutes
  8. Let cool for 1 hour before serving
