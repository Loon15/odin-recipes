Whipped Shortbread

Description

Simple, quick and fluffy shortbread that will melt in your mouth. Perfect for the holidays and pairs great with coffee or tea!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Butter
  • 1 and a 1/2 cup of Flour
  • 1/2 cup of Icing Sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325 F (160 C)
  2. Combine Butter, Sugar and Flour
  3. Mix with electric mixer for 10 minutes until light and fluffy
  4. Drop by teaspoon or icing bag onto baking sheet
  5. Bake at 325 F (160 C) for 15-20 minutes or until lightly browned
  6. remove and place on counter or rack to cool
