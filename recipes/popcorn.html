Caramel Popcorn

Description

Delicious caramel covered popcorn to satisfy that sweet tooth!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Popcorn
  • 1 cup of Butter
  • 1 cup of Brown Sugar
  • 1/2 cup of Corn Syrup
  • 1 tsp of Baking Soda

Steps

  1. Pour Corn Syrup into pot and bring to a boil
  2. Add Baking Soda to oil
  3. Add Brown Sugar and Butter
  4. Mix constantly and add your popcorn into the mixture
  5. Keep stirring until the foam cooks down
  6. Once golden brown colour remove from heat
  7. Pour mixture onto baking pan
  8. Bake for 45 min at 250 F (121 C)
